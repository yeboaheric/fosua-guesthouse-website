Our Story

A Place of
True Rest

Nestled in the heart of Aduman, Fosua Guest House is more than just a place to stay. It is a welcoming sanctuary where authentic Ghanaian culture meets exceptional hospitality. Proudly founded by the Yeboah family, every detail has been thoughtfully designed to provide guests with comfort, peace, and a true sense of home.

Whether you are a business traveller, a family on holiday, or simply exploring the beautiful Ashanti Region, Fosua Guest House offers the perfect balance of relaxation and convenience. From warm smiles and personalised service to serene surroundings and comfortable accommodations, every stay is crafted to create lasting memories.

At Fosua Guest House, you are not simply checking in — you are becoming part of a tradition of care, comfort, and genuine Ghanaian warmth.
